Learn More About EduFeedbackPRO

What is EduFeedbackPRO?

This is a centralized platform where students can view assignments, upload their work, and access important resources provided by their teachers.

Features of EduFeedbackPRO plus

  • View and download assignments uploaded by teachers.
  • Upload completed assignments for review.
  • Track grades and performance over time.
  • Contact teachers for queries and support.

How to Use EduFeedbackPRO?

Simply log in with your credentials, navigate through the menu, and access the features you need. The dashboard is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive.
